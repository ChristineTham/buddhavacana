This is a proto-Abhidhamma sutta that provides lists of related (and opposing) terms grouped by ascending numerical order (by size of list). It represents a largely TheravÄdin view of the core elements of the Buddha's teaching. The corresponding version in the Dharmaguptaka DÄ«rghÄgama - DA11 (Discourse Increasing by One) - is much shorter and better structured. The intention of the list is for communal recitation across the fourfold assembly of monastics and lay disciples.

Although some of the lists are probably derived from the Buddha's teachings, it seems some lists have been inserted at a later stage by disciples. Given these lists are presented outside the context of the Buddha's teachings, it is sometimes hard to understand the purpose and meaning for the items.
