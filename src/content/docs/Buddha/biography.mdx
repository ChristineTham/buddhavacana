This is clearly a hagiography, showing gods and deities acknowledging him, becoming divine as a result of following his teachings.

The origin of the Shakya tribe is unclear, and they have possibly mixed Aryan and indigenous lineage.[^Levman2014]) They were an eastern sub-Himalayan ethnic group living on the periphery, both geographically and culturally, of Greater Magadha.
