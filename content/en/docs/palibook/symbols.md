## Phonological Terminology

dīgha
: long

rassa
: short

garuka
: metrically long

lahuka
: metrically short

sambaddha
: connected utterance

vavatthita
: disjoint utterance

sithila
: non-aspirated stops

dhanita
: aspirated stops

niggahīta
: nasal

vimutta
: oral
