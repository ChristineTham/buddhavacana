Motivation

I have decided to attempt a new translation of KaccÄyana’s grammar, because of several reasons:

  • Unlike some translations, I am honouring the use of option markers in KaccÄyana, and in most cases am faithfully translating vÄ as “optionally”, kvaci as “occasionally” and navÄ as “by exception.”
  • I am also cross referencing the use of technical terms with other classical PÄli grammars.
  • It is a good way to further my understanding of PÄli.

  • I wanted to augment the translations with diagrams, and incorporate worked through grammatical analysis and rule derivations in the examples.
